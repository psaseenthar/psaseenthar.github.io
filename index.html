Trigger is an Osean Air Defense Force fighter pilot who was instrumental to the conclusion of the Lighthouse War. Originally part of Mage Squadron, Trigger was moved to the penal Spare Squadron after being framed by Erusean subterfuge. Following his acquital, he was given leadership of Strider Squadron.

Trigger was instrumental in destroying both Arsenal Birds captured by Erusean forces. Afterwards, he shot down the Erusean ace, Mihaly Shilage. Finally, he closed out the war by destroying two rogue drones with advanced AI, which were poised to spread themselves across the globe.
